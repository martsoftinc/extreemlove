OUR MISSION

At Extreme Love Ministries, we're here to help people move from where they are to where God wants them to be through worship, discipleship, fellowship, and outreach.

We are a Pentecostal, Bible-based, Spirit-filled, Gospel-centred, and Mission-focused community that welcomes all people.

Visit Our Branches

MEET OUR FOUNDER

BISHOP CALCULUS YEBOAH

Founder & Senior Pastor

Bishop Calculus Yeboah founded Extreme Love Ministries with a vision to create a community where God's extreme love is demonstrated through worship, fellowship, and service. With over 30 years of ministry experience, he leads with passion and dedication to spreading the Gospel.

His teachings focus on practical application of biblical principles for everyday life, empowering believers to live out their faith with purpose and passion.
